About Me

As I'm writing this, the sun scorches my back with its unbearable heat. I hate summer weather. I usually spend my summers doing art camps, reading, playing video games, and sometimes visiting Singapore. I was born in Toronto, Canada in 2004. I later moved in Chicago, Illinois, U.S.A., followed by Singapore, then Chicago. When I was younger, I wrote a lot of poetry, but then I later spent my time using Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.
